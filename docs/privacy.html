Locknotes – Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Locknotes is an Android application that lets you pin notes to your device's lock screen using persistent notifications. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Summary

Locknotes does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data. All notes you create exist only on your device. The app has no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no advertising.

Data the App Handles

The only data Locknotes works with is the content of the notes you create. This data is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage and is displayed on your lock screen via Android's notification system. Note content never leaves your device and is never sent over the internet.

Data Collection

Locknotes does not collect any information, including but not limited to:

Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number), location data, device identifiers, usage or diagnostic analytics, crash reports sent to third parties, contacts, photos, or files outside the app.

Permissions

Locknotes requests the POST_NOTIFICATIONS permission, which is required on Android 13 and later to display notifications. This permission is used solely to show your notes on the lock screen and notification shade. The app requests no other sensitive permissions.

Third-Party Services

Locknotes does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties because no data is collected.

Data Deletion

Because all data is stored locally, you are in full control of it. Deleting a note within the app removes it from your device. Uninstalling the app, or clearing the app's storage in Android settings, permanently deletes all notes. There is no copy anywhere else.

Children's Privacy

Locknotes is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children, because it does not collect data at all.

Lock Screen Visibility

Please be aware that, by design, notes you pin will be visible on your lock screen to anyone who can see your device. Avoid pinning sensitive information such as passwords if others may view your screen. You can control notification visibility on the lock screen through your device's Android settings.

Changes to This Policy

If the app's data practices ever change, this policy will be updated and the "Last updated" date revised before any new version is released.
